# GigMate Premium Fan Messaging Strategy
## Tiered Revenue Generation Through Direct Artist Access

---

## Overview

GigMate's premium fan messaging system transforms direct artist communication into a major revenue stream. By making artist access a paid premium feature while keeping venue inquiries free, we create a sustainable model that benefits all parties.

---

## Fan Messaging Tiers

### Free Tier (No Monthly Fee)

**Included:**
- ✅ Unlimited venue inquiries (always free)
- ✅ Event discovery and browsing
- ✅ Ticket purchasing
- ✅ Public artist following
- ✅ 3 free messages to any artist

**Limitations:**
- ❌ Must pay $0.99 per message after first 3
- ❌ Cannot message other fans
- ❌ No priority placement
- ❌ Standard event access only

---

### Fan Premium - $4.99/month

**Included:**
- ✅ 50 artist messages per month ($0.10 each)
- ✅ Unlimited venue inquiries
- ✅ Fan-to-fan messaging ($0.49 per message)
- ✅ Priority in artist feeds
- ✅ Early event notifications
- ✅ Profile badge

**Value:** Pay $4.99, get $50 worth of messages

---

### Fan VIP - $9.99/month

**Included:**
- ✅ **Unlimited artist messages**
- ✅ **Free fan-to-fan messaging**
- ✅ **Priority artist responses**
- ✅ Crown VIP badge
- ✅ Exclusive content access
- ✅ Early ticket access
- ✅ 10% merchandise discount
- ✅ Meet & greet opportunities

**Value:** Unlimited access for less than 11 pay-per-messages

## Implementation

**Database Tables Created:**
- `message_unlocks` - Track paid messaging access
- `message_unlock_purchases` - Purchase history
- `fan_message_packages` - Pricing options
- `fan_message_usage` - Analytics tracking

**Functions:**
- `can_fan_message_artist()` - Check messaging permissions
- `unlock_artist_messaging()` - Purchase access
- `use_message_unlock()` - Decrement usage

**UI Components:**
- Premium messaging modal with pricing
- Upgrade prompts
- VIP badge display
- Usage tracking dashboard
